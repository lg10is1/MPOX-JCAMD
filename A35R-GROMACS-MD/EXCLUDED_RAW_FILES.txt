Excluded from the public code/results package:
- Raw and processed trajectories: *.xtc, *.trr
- Binary run files and checkpoints: *.tpr, *.edr, *.cpt
- Large coordinate/topology/index files: *.gro, *.top, *.ndx, *.itp
- Full GROMACS logs: *.log
- Raw XVG files with command/path headers: *.xvg
- Optional trajectory archive folder: 11_optional_trajectories

These files were omitted because they are very large, environment-specific, or contain private filesystem/cluster metadata.
The analysis code, parameter files, summarized CSV tables, per-replicate CSV exports, and figure files are retained.
